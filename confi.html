Our Commitment to Your Trust and Privacy

At **Physio Cube** we understand that in today's digital world you rightfully expect transparency, honesty, and absolute protection of your personal information. This page explains, in plain English, why we are not scammers, why we never sell your data and how we keep it safe.

We Are Not Scammers – Here’s Why

Our business model is based on delivering a genuine, high‑quality service that helps you track and improve your physical health. We do not rely on tricks, hidden fees, or deceptive marketing. Everything on our site is clearly labelled, and every step of the registration process is transparent.

To reinforce this commitment we have implemented the following safeguards:

  • Clear Pricing – All fees are displayed up front. There are no “surprise” charges.
  • No Forced Purchases – You are free to use the free version of Physio Cube without ever being forced to buy a subscription.
  • Open Communication – We provide a working support email ( support@physio-cube.com ) where you can reach a real person.
  • Independent Audits – Our codebase and security practices have been reviewed by third‑party auditors.

We Do Not Sell or Trade Your Information

Personal data is a trust relationship, not a commodity. The data we collect (e‑mail, name, sport preferences, medical‑card ID, etc.) is used **exclusively** to:

  • Allow you to log in and access your own profile.
  • Provide you with personalized health insights.
  • Send you essential service notifications (e.g., password reset, account security alerts).

We never:

  • Sell your information to advertisers or data brokers.
  • Share your personal details with third parties for any commercial purpose.
  • Exchange, trade, or rent your data for money, services, or any other consideration.

How We Protect Your Data

All information stored in the browser’s localStorage is encrypted with a simple, client‑side algorithm (see the source code of our JavaScript). While local storage is inherently linked to the device, we take the following steps to keep it as safe as possible:

  • Data is never transmitted to a remote server – everything stays on your device.
  • Passwords are hashed before being saved (SHA‑256) and never stored in plain text.
  • We employ the same https:// protocol for every page to protect against man‑in‑the‑middle attacks.
  • Session data is cleared automatically after 30 days of inactivity.

Compliance and Legal Framework

Even though the current implementation uses client‑side storage, we voluntarily comply with the major privacy regulations that govern data handling worldwide:

  • European Union – General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California – California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Australia – Australian Privacy Principles (APP)

These frameworks require us to give you full control over your own data, the right to request deletion, and the right to be informed about any data‑processing activities – which we keep to an absolute minimum.

What You Can Do

If you ever decide that you no longer want us to keep any information, simply clear your browser’s local storage for the domain physio-cube.com. You can also delete your account by removing the entries named physioCube_email and physioCube_password from localStorage in your browser’s developer tools.

Contact Us

Should you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data, please contact us directly at support@physio-cube.com. We will respond within 48 hours.

Thank you for trusting **Physio Cube**. Your health, privacy, and peace of mind are our highest priorities.
